## Quick Answer

**Decaf coffee contains roughly 2–15mg of caffeine per 8oz cup.** The exact amount varies by brand, brewing method, and decaffeination process. It is not caffeine-free.

---

## The Surprising Truth About Decaf

Most people switch to decaf assuming it's a caffeine-free option. It isn't. Decaffeination removes around 97% of the caffeine — but that remaining 3% adds up, especially if you're having two or three cups in the afternoon.

The FDA requires decaf to have "substantially less" caffeine than regular coffee, but sets no firm lower limit. That leaves a wide range depending on the brand and process.

## Caffeine in Decaf vs Regular Coffee

| Drink | Caffeine per 8oz cup |
|---|---|
| Brewed regular coffee | 80–100mg |
| Espresso (1 shot / 1oz) | 63mg |
| Starbucks Decaf Pike Place | ~25mg |
| Typical supermarket decaf | 2–15mg |
| Swiss Water Process decaf | 0–3mg |
| Instant decaf | 2–12mg |

Starbucks decaf is notably higher than home-brewed decaf — a large cup can contain as much as 30mg. If you're sensitive to caffeine, that matters.

## Does Decaf Affect Your Cut-Off Time?

For most people, 10–15mg of caffeine won't meaningfully shift your sleep. But if you're sensitive to caffeine, have a low bodyweight, are pregnant, or are already close to your daily limit — even decaf in the evening adds to the total.

## Which Decaffeination Method Has the Least Caffeine?

There are three main methods:

- **Swiss Water Process** — uses only water, no solvents. Removes up to 99.9% of caffeine. Closest to truly caffeine-free.
- **CO₂ Process** — high-pressure CO₂ strips caffeine with minimal flavour loss. Around 96–99% removed.
- **Chemical solvent process** (methylene chloride or ethyl acetate) — standard commercial method, removes around 97%.

If you're switching to decaf specifically to reduce caffeine, look for Swiss Water Process on the label.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is decaf coffee completely caffeine-free?
No. Decaf coffee typically contains 2–15mg of caffeine per 8oz cup. The decaffeination process removes around 97% of the original caffeine, but a small amount always remains.

### How much caffeine is in Starbucks decaf?
A Starbucks Decaf Pike Place (grande, 16oz) contains approximately 25mg of caffeine — higher than most home-brewed decaf.

### Can decaf coffee keep you awake?
For most people, the 2–15mg in a typical decaf is too small to noticeably affect sleep. However, people with high caffeine sensitivity or low bodyweight may notice a difference, particularly with multiple cups.

### What is Swiss Water Process decaf?
Swiss Water Process is a decaffeination method that uses only water to remove caffeine, with no chemical solvents. It removes up to 99.9% of caffeine and is the closest commercially available option to fully caffeine-free coffee.

### How does decaf compare to regular coffee in caffeine?
Regular brewed coffee contains 80–100mg per 8oz cup. Decaf contains roughly 2–15mg — around 97% less. Espresso contains 63mg per single shot.
