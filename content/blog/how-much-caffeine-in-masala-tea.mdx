## Quick Answer

**Masala tea (masala chai) contains approximately 25–50mg of caffeine per 8oz cup**, depending on how strong it's brewed, the black tea variety used, and the ratio of milk to tea.

---

## What Is Masala Tea and Why Does It Contain Caffeine?

Masala chai is spiced black tea — typically brewed with cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, and black pepper, then simmered with milk. The caffeine comes entirely from the black tea base. The spices contain no caffeine.

The caffeine level varies based on:
- **Brew strength and steep time** — longer steeping extracts more caffeine
- **Tea variety** — Assam tea (common in chai) is higher in caffeine than Darjeeling
- **Loose leaf vs tea bag** — loose leaf tends to deliver more caffeine
- **Milk ratio** — diluting with more milk slightly reduces total caffeine per cup

## Masala Tea vs Other Drinks: Caffeine Comparison

| Drink | Caffeine per 8oz |
|---|---|
| Brewed coffee | 80–100mg |
| Masala chai (home-brewed) | 25–50mg |
| Chai tea latte (Starbucks, grande) | 95mg |
| Black tea (standard) | 40–70mg |
| Green tea | 20–45mg |
| Matcha (8oz prepared) | 40–70mg |
| Decaf coffee | 2–15mg |

Note: a Starbucks Chai Tea Latte uses a pre-made concentrate with added sugars — the caffeine is comparable to a cup of coffee and much higher than home-brewed masala chai.

## How Masala Tea Affects Your Caffeine Cut-Off

At 25–50mg per cup, masala chai is significantly lower than coffee. Two cups in the afternoon is roughly equivalent to half a cup of coffee. For most people this is manageable — but if you're sensitive to caffeine or already close to your daily limit, timing still matters.

## Tips for Reducing Caffeine in Masala Chai

- Steep for a shorter time (2–3 minutes instead of 5+)
- Use a lower-caffeine base like green tea or rooibos
- Increase the milk ratio
- Use decaf black tea as the base — you keep all the spice flavour with near-zero caffeine

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does masala chai have caffeine?
Yes. Masala chai contains caffeine from its black tea base — typically 25–50mg per 8oz cup when home-brewed. The spices (cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, cloves) contain no caffeine.

### Is masala chai high in caffeine compared to coffee?
No. Home-brewed masala chai typically contains 25–50mg per cup versus 80–100mg in brewed coffee. However, a Starbucks Chai Tea Latte can reach 95mg due to the concentrated syrup.

### Can I drink masala tea at night?
For most people, one cup of home-brewed masala chai in the evening is unlikely to significantly affect sleep. However, if you're caffeine-sensitive, it's worth calculating your personal cut-off time based on your bodyweight and bedtime.

### How much caffeine is in a Starbucks Chai Tea Latte?
A Starbucks Chai Tea Latte (grande, 16oz) contains approximately 95mg of caffeine — much higher than home-brewed masala chai and comparable to a cup of coffee.

### Which has more caffeine — masala chai or green tea?
It depends on preparation, but they're broadly similar. Masala chai runs 25–50mg per 8oz; green tea runs 20–45mg. Home-brewed masala chai can edge higher with a strong steep.
